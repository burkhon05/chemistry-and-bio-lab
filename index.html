About the Lab

The Placeholder Research Group investigates host–microbiome interactions, immune system regulation, and computational modeling in early development and disease. Our work combines experimental immunology, microbiome profiling, and data-driven approaches to uncover new biological insights.

Featured Projects

  • Microbiome & Brain Development: Studying how early-life gut microbes shape neural outcomes
  • Inflammation & Cancer: Mapping neutrophil behavior in tumor microenvironments
  • AI in Immunology: Using machine learning to decode immune cell trajectories
